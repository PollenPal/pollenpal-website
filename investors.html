The problem

Bees feed the world. But bees are dying faster than food demand is growing.

US beekeepers lose about 40% of their colonies a year (48% in 2023). Replacement costs run $2.5B+ globally. The workforce is aging. The median commercial beekeeper is 66, and 79% use no digital management tools at all. The industry is ready for a reset.

The solution

Physical AI for every hive.

01 · HARDWARE

Watches every bee in and out

Off-the-shelf compute. Solar plus LTE for remote yards. Drops in under standard 10-frame Langstroth boxes. No custom hive bodies.

02 · MACHINE LEARNING

YOLOv11 on 20k+ field images

Tracks colony activity in real time. Detects mites at 1 to 1.5mm, africanization markers, and missing-queen indicators. Cloud inference today, central GPU on the roadmap.

03 · WEB APP

Hive-level alerts, multi-yard views

Designed with beekeeper input. Actionable insights over complex graphs. Recordkeeping, harvest logging, supply ordering integrated.
