Built for backyard beekeeping

Designed for hobbyists, not for fleets.

SETUP

Plugs into your house

Home WiFi and a regular power outlet. No solar panels, no LTE, no cellular plan. You can have it running in minutes.

SUPPORT

Snap a photo, get a diagnosis

The AI assistant tells you what you're looking at. When you need a real human, the optional live beekeeper chat connects you with someone who's seen it before.

SHARE

Live stream your hive

Share your hive entrance with friends, your local bee club, or the internet. Watching bees come and go is one of the best parts of beekeeping.

Watch and share

Bring your hive to the couch.

PollenPal turns your phone into a window into the colony. Open the app from work, from your kitchen, from your friend's backyard. Your hive is right there.

LIVE

24/7 entrance feed

A live stream of every bee coming and going. Watch the morning rush, the evening calm, the foragers piling in on a good goldenrod day. Real time, every day, no schedule.

INSIDE

Look inside the hive

Scheduled views from inside the box. See the brood pattern grow, the queen lay, the drones wander. All without opening the lid and stressing the colony.

SHARE

Send the moments worth showing off

Save a clip, send a swarm capture to your bee club, post a 30-second loop of forager traffic. Your hive, your audience. The best part of beekeeping made easy to share.
