Questions

Good to know.

Do I need the PollenPal device?

No. The app works entirely on its own. The device is a separate product; this is just the inspection software, and it runs on the phone in your pocket.

What do I need to get started?

A phone and your hives. Sign up, add your hives, and log your first inspection in a couple of minutes.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. Cancel whenever you like. If you cancel during the free trial you are never charged.

Is my data mine?

Yes. Your hives and your inspection records belong to you.
